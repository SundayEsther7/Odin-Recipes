Perfect Summer Fruit Salad

Description

This fruit salad is perfect for a backyard bbq or any occasion.

There are never leftovers! This is one of my favorite fruit salad recipes, as I think the citrusy sauce really makes it.

This salad is tastier the longer you can let it soak in its juices.

I prefer 3 to 4 hours in the refrigerator before I serve it.

    Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Additional Time: 3 hrs Total Time: 3 hrs 30 mins Servings: 10

Ingridients

For the sause:

    fresh orange and lemon juices, brown sugar, grated orange and lemon zests, and vanilla extract

The fruits you will need

    pineapple, strawberries, kiwis, bananas, oranges, grapes, and blueberries

Steps

    1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld.
